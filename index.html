SEO (Search Engine Optimisation)

SEO is all about improving the visibility of your website or the pages on your website for specific keywords or keyword phrases in natural search engine search listings. Natural results are also known by different names such as “organic,” “unpaid,” and “earned” results. In general, higher ranking and more frequent appearance of your website or web pages in search results pages or lists drive more visitors to your business. SEO can also be employed to target images, locality, videos, and news, among others.

Search engine optimization is an important strategy as far as online marketing is concerned. At Codvox, an SEO company, we employ both onsite and off-site strategies to ensure top results for you.
